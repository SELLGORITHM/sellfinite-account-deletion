Note: Currently, account deletion is processed manually via email. In-app account deletion is coming in a future update.

How to Request Account Deletion:

Email Request Process

1. Send an email to: sellgorithmenquiry@gmail.com

2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

3. Include your registered phone number in the email

4. We will confirm deletion within 48 hours

What Gets Deleted:

  • User profile information
  • Property listings (if any)
  • Chat history and messages
  • Search preferences and history

What May Be Retained (for legal compliance):

  • Transaction records (retained for 90 days)
  • Anonymous analytics data

Processing Time: Account deletion requests are processed within 48 hours of email receipt.
